Info

My name is Elijah Medina, I am a 21 year old Computer Science major from Lansing, Michigan. I currently go to school at Michigan State University, and am currently in my fourth year! I've lived in Lansing for my whole life, but I have some family in Florida as well. I also love to draw! Here you can see some of my artwork!

I like to make drawings in Clip Studio Paint, and will either draw with my mouse, or I draw will my tablet and stylus. I also have a few interests and hobbies other than drawing, heres a few!
